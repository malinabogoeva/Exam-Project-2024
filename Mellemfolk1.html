About Mellemfolk

Café Mellemfolk is a unique café in Aarhus that combines a relaxed atmosphere with a focus on cultural diversity and social causes. With an emphasis on sustainability, the café serves Fairtrade coffee, organic teas, and local, vegan-friendly snacks. The space encourages community engagement, hosting events such as open discussions, workshops, and live performances. It's more than just a café—it's a hub for like-minded individuals to connect and support global initiatives.

Tips!

Plan Your Visit: Explore Before You Order: Walk around and check all the food stands before deciding. There’s something for every taste!

Explore the Stalls: Timing is Key: Visit during off-peak hours for shorter lines, or come in the evening for a lively atmosphere.

Bring Cash: Seating Strategy: Find a table before ordering, especially during busy times.

Try the Food: Eco-Friendly Practices: Support sustainability by properly sorting your waste into the provided bins.
